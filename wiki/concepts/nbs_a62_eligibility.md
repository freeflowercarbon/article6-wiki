# NBS × Article 6.2 Eligibility Guide | NBS × Article 6.2 適格性ガイド

*最終更新: 2026-05-11*

=== "🇬🇧 English"

    ## Overview

    Nature-Based Solutions (NBS) represent the largest pipeline of potential ITMOs under Article 6.2. However, eligibility is determined by a layered set of requirements: universal host-country obligations, methodology quality signals (primarily the ICVCM Core Carbon Principles / CCP label), and acquiring-country-specific rules. This page provides a comprehensive map of which NBS project types and methodologies currently qualify — or are on a path to qualifying — for A6.2.

    > **Scope note:** Rice Methane / AWD is treated separately at the bottom of this page, as it is an agricultural methane-avoidance activity rather than a traditional land-based NBS.

    ---

    ## 1. Universal A6.2 Requirements (All Activity Types)

    All NBS projects must satisfy these four requirements regardless of methodology or acquiring country:

    | # | Requirement | Description |
    |---|-------------|-------------|
    | 1 | **Host country authorization** | The host government must formally authorize the specific project/credits as ITMOs via national registry |
    | 2 | **Corresponding Adjustment (CA)** | Host country deducts transferred volume from its own NDC accounting → prevents double counting |
    | 3 | **Bilateral Implementation Agreement (IA)** | A signed IA between host and acquiring country is required; sets rules for transfer, cancellation, and reporting |
    | 4 | **Acquiring country quality requirements** | Each acquiring country sets its own quality bar (CCP, cancellation %, adaptation levy, etc.) |

    Requirements 1–3 are fixed for all A6.2 transactions. Requirement 4 varies by buyer — see Section 4.

    ## 5. JCM Special Case

    Japan's Joint Crediting Mechanism (JCM) operates under a fundamentally different framework from standard A6.2 ITMOs:

    | Feature | Standard A6.2 | JCM |
    |---------|---------------|-----|
    | Methodology | Open (ICVCM CCP preferred by buyers) | Pre-approved JCM methodology list per country |
    | CCP requirement | Buyer-dependent (SG/CH require CCP) | Not required — JCM has own quality review |
    | Project registration | Host country national registry | JCM Joint Committee (bilateral) |
    | NBS coverage | REDD+, ARR, Blue Carbon | Primarily energy/industry; NBS expanding |
    | CA mechanism | Mandatory per Paris Agreement | Applied via JCM bilateral agreement |
    | Key NBS examples | — | Rice paddy AWD (Philippines PH_AM004), agroforestry (Indonesia) |

    > JCM credits from NBS projects can qualify as A6.2 ITMOs if the bilateral JCM agreement includes CA provisions — which Japan has been implementing since 2023.

    ## 6. Rice Methane / AWD (Separate Treatment)

    Rice paddy methane avoidance via Alternate Wetting and Drying (AWD) is an **agricultural methane-avoidance** activity, distinct from traditional NBS. It is included here because it is frequently bundled with NBS investment discussions and has active A6.2 pipelines.

    | Methodology | Standard | CCP Status | A6.2 Potential | Notes |
    |-------------|----------|-----------|----------------|-------|
    | GS Methane Emission Reduction by AWD v1.0 | Gold Standard | ✅ **CCP Approved** (Feb 2026)[^ccp_rice_gs] | ✅ High | First rice methane methodology to receive CCP label |
    | VM0051 Improved Management in Rice Production Systems v1.0 | Verra VCS | 🟡 Under ICVCM assessment[^vm0051] | 🟡 If approved | Next in queue; result expected 2026 |
    | PH_AM004 | JCM Philippines | JCM-approved (Feb 3, 2025)[^jcm_ph_rice] | ✅ JCM path | Approved by Japan-Philippines JCM Joint Committee; Kanematsu/Green Carbon projects active |

    **Singapore compatibility:** Singapore's IA with the Philippines is not yet signed; GS AWD methodology is CCP-approved, making it eligible once an IA is in place.
    **JCM Philippines:** The most active immediate pathway — PH_AM004 approved, projects underway (Green Carbon: ~1 Mt over 10 years in Leyte).

=== "🇯🇵 日本語"

    ## 概要

    Nature-Based Solutions（NBS）はArticle 6.2のITMOパイプラインの中で最大規模を占める。ただし適格性の判断は多層的な要件によって決まる：ホスト国の義務（共通）、方法論の品質シグナル（主にICVCMのCCPラベル）、そして調達国ごとの独自要件。このページはどのNBS活動タイプ・方法論が現在A6.2適格であるか、または適格に向かっているかを体系的に整理する。

    > **対象範囲の注記：** Rice Methane / AWD（水田メタン削減）は伝統的なNBSとは異なる農業メタン回避活動であるため、このページの末尾（セクション6）で独立して扱う。

    ---

    ## 1. A6.2共通要件（全活動タイプ）

    方法論・調達国を問わず、全NBSプロジェクトが満たすべき4要件：

    | # | 要件 | 内容 |
    |---|------|------|
    | 1 | **ホスト国授権** | ホスト国政府が当該プロジェクト/クレジットをITMOとして国家登録簿上で正式に授権 |
    | 2 | **Corresponding Adjustment（CA）** | ホスト国が移転量を自国NDC会計から差し引く → ダブルカウント防止 |
    | 3 | **二国間実施協定（IA）** | ホスト国と調達国の間でIAが署名済みであること |
    | 4 | **調達国品質要件** | 各調達国が独自の品質基準を設定（CCP、取消義務、適応貢献等） |

    要件1〜3は全A6.2取引に固定。要件4は買国によって異なる（セクション4参照）。

    ## 5. JCM特殊ケース

    JCMは標準的なA6.2 ITMOとは根本的に異なるフレームワークで運用される：

    | 比較項目 | 標準A6.2 | JCM |
    |---------|----------|-----|

    | CCP要件 | 買国依存（SG/CHは必須） | 不要（JCM独自品質審査） |
    | プロジェクト登録 | ホスト国国家登録簿 | JCM合同委員会（二国間） |
    | NBS対象 | REDD+、ARR、ブルーカーボン等 | 主にエネルギー・産業；NBS拡大中 |
    | CA | パリ協定上の義務として適用 | JCM二国間協定に基づいて適用 |
    | NBS主要例 | — | 水田AWD（フィリピン PH_AM004）、農業林業（インドネシア）等 |

    ## 6. Rice Methane / AWD（独立セクション）

    水田からのメタン排出削減（AWD等）は農業メタン回避活動であり、伝統的なNBSとは区別される。

    | 方法論 | 規格 | CCP状況 | A6.2可能性 | 備考 |
    |--------|------|---------|-----------|------|
    | GS Methane Emission Reduction by AWD v1.0 | Gold Standard | ✅ **CCP承認済み**（2026年2月）[^ccp_rice_gs] | ✅ 高い | 初のRice Methane CCP承認方法論 |
    | VM0051 Improved Management in Rice Production Systems v1.0 | Verra VCS | 🟡 ICVCM審査中[^vm0051] | 🟡 承認後 | 次の候補；2026年中に結果見込み |
    | PH_AM004 | JCM（フィリピン） | JCM承認済み（2025年2月3日）[^jcm_ph_rice] | ✅ JCM経路 | Kanematsu・Green Carbon等で実証開始 |

    **シンガポールとの関係：** GS AWDはCCP承認済みだが、シンガポール-フィリピンIA未締結のため現時点では契約不可。IA締結後は即座に適格。
    **JCMフィリピン：** PH_AM004承認済み、Green Carbonがレイテ島で4万haの水田を活用し10年間で約100万tのクレジット生成を計画中。
