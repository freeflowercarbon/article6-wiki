# Côte d'Ivoire 🇨🇮 | コートジボワール — Article 6.2 Host Country

=== "🇬🇧 English"

    ## Overview

    Côte d'Ivoire is a Japan JCM partner (2021) and West Africa's largest economy (world's #1 cocoa producer). It holds significant NBS potential combining remaining tropical forest with cocoa agroforestry — a unique sustainable agriculture carbon opportunity. Historical deforestation has been severe (>90% loss over 100 years) but the government's 2030 Vision includes forest restoration, creating a significant REDD+ pipeline if governance improves.

    ## Cocoa × Carbon Opportunity

    - **REDD+ + Cocoa**: Conglomerate approach (Rainforest Alliance etc.) combining carbon credits and sustainable certification
    - **West Africa Forest Initiative (WAFI concept)**: Similar to Central Africa's CAFI, a pan-West Africa forest finance initiative is under discussion
    - **Carbon additionality**: Cocoa farming expansion drives deforestation — REDD+ programs that change incentive structures are genuinely additional

=== "🇯🇵 日本語"

    ## サマリー
    日本JCM（2021年）パートナー。西アフリカ最大の経済国（カカオ生産世界1位）で、熱帯雨林とカカオアグロフォレストリーを組み合わせたNBS資源を持つ。森林減少が深刻（過去100年で90%以上減少）だが、2030年ビジョンで森林回復を掲げており、保全型炭素の供給ポテンシャルがある。
