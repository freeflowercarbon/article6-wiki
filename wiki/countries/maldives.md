# Maldives 🇲🇻 | モルディブ — Article 6.2 Host Country

=== "🇬🇧 English"

    ## Overview

    Maldives is a Japan JCM partner (2013) — a small island developing state at the climate change frontline. Sea level rise and coral reef degradation are the primary threats. JCM projects replace diesel generation with solar on inhabited islands and improve resort hotel efficiency. As a credit supplier for A6.2, Maldives' role is limited by its tiny territory and emissions; its primary role is as a climate vulnerability advocate.

    ## サマリー
    日本JCM（2013年）パートナー。気候変動の最前線に立つ小島嶼国で、海面上昇への適応が最優先課題。JCMは島嶼のディーゼル発電を太陽光に置き換える案件・リゾートホテルの省エネ化が中心。A6.2ホスト国としての役割は限定的。
