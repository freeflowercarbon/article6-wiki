# Maldives 🇲🇻 | モルディブ — Article 6.2 Host Country

=== "🇬🇧 English"

    ## Overview

    Maldives is a Japan JCM partner (2013) — a small island developing state at the climate change frontline. On **December 17, 2025, Maldives and Japan completed the second-ever ITMO transfer under the JCM** ("Smart Mini Grid System at Addu City", MV002 — 433 tCO₂e, vintage 2021–2022, acquired by Japan's Ministry of the Environment).[^mvitmo] JCM projects replace diesel generation with solar on inhabited islands and improve resort hotel efficiency. As a credit supplier, Maldives' volume is limited by its tiny territory; its primary role is as a climate vulnerability advocate.

=== "🇯🇵 日本語"

    ## サマリー
    日本JCM（2013年）パートナー。**2025年12月17日、JCM史上2件目のITMO移転を日本との間で完了**（アッドゥ市スマートミニグリッド MV002、433t、2021〜22ビンテージ、環境省取得）[^mvitmo]。気候変動の最前線に立つ小島嶼国で、海面上昇への適応が最優先課題。JCMは島嶼のディーゼル発電を太陽光に置き換える案件・リゾートホテルの省エネ化が中心。供給量は国土の制約から限定的。
