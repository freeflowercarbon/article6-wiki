# Uzbekistan 🇺🇿 | ウズベキスタン — Article 6.2 Host Country

=== "🇬🇧 English"

    ## Overview

    Uzbekistan is a Japan JCM partner (2016) — Central Asia's most populous country and a major agricultural producer. JCM projects focus on district heating modernization and industrial energy efficiency. The unique context of **Aral Sea restoration** (world's largest environmental rehabilitation effort) gives Uzbekistan a distinctive NBS narrative, though methodologies for crediting such work remain to be developed.
