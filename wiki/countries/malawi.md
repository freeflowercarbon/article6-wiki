# Malawi 🇲🇼 | マラウィ — Article 6.2 Host Country

=== "🇬🇧 English"

    ## Overview

    Malawi is an **active Article 6.2 pipeline country** with a bilateral agreement with Switzerland and a formal Article 6.2 Initial Report (AIR) submitted to the UNFCCC in August 2025 — one of only a handful of countries globally to have reached this stage. The primary authorized project is a **clean cooking intervention** (Chitetezo Mbaula efficient biomass stoves), implemented by the Hestian Carbon Fund under the KliK obligation framework. Malawi's NDC targets span to 2040 with significant AFOLU potential, though Switzerland's exclusion of NBS/forestry from its ITMO eligibility means the near-term pipeline is focused on clean cooking and energy efficiency — not the country's large forest carbon stock.

    ## NDC Achievement Status

    > Malawi's NDC extends to **2040** (unusual — most NDCs target 2030), which gives a longer implementation window but reduces near-term urgency for credit buyers assessing NDC overselling risk. Key mitigation sectors: AFOLU (forest/agriculture), Energy. The conditional target (51%) requires substantial international finance — Article 6.2 revenue is intended to catalyze this gap.[^mwndcstatus]

    | Metric | Assessment |

    ## NBS Resources — Switzerland Eligibility Note

    > ⚠️ **Critical constraint for NBS investors**: Switzerland's CO2 Act explicitly **blacklists land use, forestry (including REDD+), and biological CO₂ sequestration from international ITMO eligibility**. Malawi's large forest carbon stock (~60 Mt CO₂e restoration potential) and REDD+ pipeline cannot be sold to Switzerland under Article 6.2. NBS project developers must target other acquiring countries (Singapore, Japan JCM, Sweden) for ITMO off-take — none of which currently have an active bilateral with Malawi.[^mwchexcl]

=== "🇯🇵 日本語"

    ## サマリー
    マラウィはスイスとの二国間協定を締結し、2025年8月にUNFCCCへArticle 6.2初期報告書（AIR）を提出した**アクティブなパイプライン国**。承認済みの主要プロジェクトはクリーンクッキング（Chitetezo Mbaula効率的バイオマス調理炉）で、KliKのHestian Carbon Fundが実施。NDCは2040年目標（珍しく長期設定）で、大規模な森林・土地利用炭素ポテンシャルを持つが、スイスの土地利用・林業除外ポリシーにより、NBS/REDD+クレジットはスイス向けITMOとして不適格。

    ## NBS投資家向け注記

    > ⚠️ **重要制約**: スイスのCO2法は、**土地利用・林業（REDD+含む）・生物的CO₂吸収プロジェクトを海外ITMOとして明示的に除外**している。マラウィの大規模な森林炭素ポテンシャル（約6,000万tCO₂e）はスイス向けには販売不可。NBS開発者はシンガポール・日本JCM・スウェーデン等の他の調達国を探索する必要があるが、現時点でマラウィとの二国間IAを持つ国はない。
