# Mexico 🇲🇽 | メキシコ — Article 6.2 Host Country

=== "🇬🇧 English"

    ## Overview

    Mexico is a Japan JCM partner (2014) and one of Latin America's largest economies. It piloted a domestic ETS in 2020 and has a carbon tax. Mexico holds substantial NBS resources (Chiapas tropical forests, Yucatán mangroves). Climate policy experienced setbacks under the 2018–2024 López Obrador administration, but the new government (2024) has signaled re-engagement with climate priorities.
