# Moldova 🇲🇩 | モルドバ — Article 6.2 Host Country

=== "🇬🇧 English"

    ## Overview

    Moldova is a Japan JCM partner (2016) and an **EU accession candidate** (granted in 2022). As Moldova aligns its systems with EU standards, its priority is EU ETS compatibility rather than bilateral A6.2 agreements. JCM focuses on small-scale energy efficiency and agricultural projects. Russia-to-EU energy transition creates JCM opportunities (RE independence). JCM activity is expected to gradually reduce as EU accession progresses.

    | Item | Detail |
    |------|--------|
    | NDC Target | 70% reduction vs. 1990 by 2030 (EU-aligned) |
    | Carbon System | Exploring EU ETS alignment |
    | Key Ministry | Ministry of Environment |
    | JCM (Japan) | ✅ Since 2016 |
    | CA Framework | 🔴 EU alignment takes priority |
    | NBS Maturity | ⭐⭐ |

    ## Key Considerations

    - **EU accession**: EU ETS introduction may reduce relevance of A6.2 bilateral agreements
    - **Russia energy independence**: RE development accelerating — JCM opportunity
    - **Ukraine conflict impact**: Geopolitical risks in the neighborhood
