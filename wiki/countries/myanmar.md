# Myanmar 🇲🇲 | ミャンマー — Article 6.2 Host Country

=== "🇬🇧 English"

    ## Overview

    Myanmar is a Japan JCM partner (2014) but **all JCM activities have been effectively suspended** since the military coup of February 2021. Myanmar holds substantial NBS resources (tropical forests, Irrawaddy mangroves) but the political situation, international sanctions, and governance collapse have made carbon market investment highly inadvisable.

    | Item | Detail |
    |------|--------|
    | NDC Target | Reduction vs. 2000 baseline (updates stalled due to political crisis) |
    | Carbon System | None (development halted) |
    | Key Ministry | Ministry of Natural Resources and Environmental Conservation (under military government) |
    | JCM (Japan) | ⚠️ Since 2014 — activities suspended |
    | Singapore IA | ⚪ None |
    | CA Framework | None |
    | NBS Maturity | ⭐ (rich resources; institutions non-functional) |

    ## Investment Risk Warning

    !!! danger "Not investable at present"
        Since the February 2021 coup, major democracies have maintained economic sanctions and engagement restrictions. Japan's government has suspended new JCM project approvals. Carbon credit investment carries extreme political risk and is not recommended.

    ## サマリー
    日本JCM（2014年）パートナーだが、2021年2月の軍事クーデター以降JCM活動は事実上停止中。豊富な熱帯雨林・マングローブというNBS資源を保有するが、政情不安と国際制裁により炭素市場活動が著しく制限されている。

    ## 投資家向け注意事項

    !!! danger "現時点で投資不可"
        2021年クーデター以降、主要民主主義国は経済制裁・関与自粛を維持。日本政府もJCMの新規プロジェクト承認を停止中。炭素クレジット投資は政治リスクが極めて高く、現時点では推奨されない。
