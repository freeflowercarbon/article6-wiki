# Tunisia 🇹🇳 | チュニジア — Article 6.2 Host Country

=== "🇬🇧 English"

    ## Overview

    Tunisia is a Japan JCM partner (2015) and a **Switzerland bilateral partner** — a North African country with strong solar and wind resources. JCM and Switzerland bilateral projects focus primarily on renewable energy (large-scale solar, energy efficiency). If the ELMED submarine power cable (planned link to Italy) becomes operational, green electricity export to Europe could generate significant additional carbon credit opportunities.
