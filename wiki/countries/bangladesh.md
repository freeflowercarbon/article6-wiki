# Bangladesh 🇧🇩 | バングラデシュ — Article 6.2 Host Country

=== "🇬🇧 English"

    ## Overview

    Bangladesh is a Japan JCM partner (2013) and one of the world's **most climate-vulnerable countries**. Adaptation is the primary priority. The **Sundarbans** (world's largest mangrove forest, shared with India) is Bangladesh's signature NBS asset. JCM focuses on industrial energy efficiency and RE. A6.2 institutional development is at an early stage, with limited government engagement in carbon markets so far.

    | Coastal restoration | ⭐⭐ | Integrated with cyclone/flood adaptation |

    ## Risk Factors

    - **Climate vulnerability**: Sea level rise and cyclones create long-term NBS permanence risk (flood-vulnerable mangroves)
    - **Institutional capacity**: Limited government engagement in carbon markets; technical capacity development needed
