# Kazakhstan 🇰🇿 | カザフスタン — Article 6.2 Host Country

=== "🇬🇧 English"

    ## Overview

    Kazakhstan is a Japan JCM partner (2014) and the first CIS (former Soviet Union) country to operate a mandatory **Emissions Trading Scheme (KZ-ETS, since 2013)**. The primary emission sources are oil, gas, and coal industries. JCM focuses on industrial energy efficiency and flaring reduction. Steppe and grassland NBS potential is under exploration but at early research stage.

    | Item | Detail |

    | Carbon System | KZ-ETS (2013–); one of Asia's first mandatory ETS |

    ## サマリー
    日本JCM（2014年）パートナー。CIS（旧ソ連）諸国で最初に本格的な国内ETS（KZ-ETS、2013年〜）を導入した国。石油・ガス・石炭産業が主要排出源で、工業省エネ・フレアリング削減が主なJCMの対象。草原・ステップのNBS可能性も検討段階。
